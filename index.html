Welcome to Talent Nexa

India’s premier talent search partner, empowering companies to connect with next-generation talent and drive innovation.

Founded in 2023, Talent Nexa Consulting is India’s trusted talent search partner, connecting organizations with next-gen talent. With over two decades of combined experience in executive search and deep-tech hiring, we specialize in leadership & lateral roles across technology-driven domain's like E-commerce, FinTech, EdTech, AI/ML, SaaS, Logistics, Cyber Security, Semiconductor and more. Our people-first, technology-forward approach ensures that every hire aligns with your culture, vision, and growth goals. At Talent Nexa, we’re committed to helping companies build teams that shape the future of work.
